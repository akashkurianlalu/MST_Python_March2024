Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Additional butter or oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Batter: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Combine Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Mix Batter: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined.
  4. Heat the Pan: Place a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add a small amount of butter or oil to coat the surface.
  5. Cook the Pancakes: Pour about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles form on the surface.
  6. Flip and Cook: Carefully flip the pancake and cook until golden brown on the other side.
  7. Serve: Transfer the cooked pancakes to a plate. Repeat with the remaining batter.

Cooking Time: Approximately 15 minutes total, depending on batch size.

Serving Size: Makes about 8 medium-sized pancakes, serving 2-4 people.

Enjoy your pancakes!
