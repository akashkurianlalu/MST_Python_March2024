Chicken Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded or diced
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt (or sour cream)
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Lettuce leaves, for serving
  • Sliced tomatoes and cucumber, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Cook and shred or dice the chicken. Chop celery, red onion, and parsley.
  2. Make Dressing: In a bowl, mix together mayonnaise, Greek yogurt (or sour cream), lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Combine Salad: In a large bowl, combine chicken, celery, red onion, and parsley.
  4. Add Dressing: Pour the dressing over the chicken mixture. Stir until well combined.
  5. Chill: Cover and refrigerate the chicken salad for at least 1 hour before serving to allow flavors to meld.
  6. Serve: Arrange lettuce leaves on plates. Spoon chicken salad onto lettuce. Garnish with sliced tomatoes and cucumber if desired.

Serving Size: Makes about 4 servings.

Note: Feel free to customize the salad with additional ingredients like diced apples, grapes, or nuts.

Enjoy your chicken salad!
