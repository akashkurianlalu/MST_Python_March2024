Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 3/4 cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup (65g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and 3/4 cups (350g) granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) boiling water
  • Chocolate frosting or ganache, for topping (optional)
  • Fresh berries or sprinkles, for decoration (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch (23cm) round cake pans.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add Wet Ingredients: Add eggs, buttermilk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.
  4. Add Boiling Water: Gradually add boiling water to the batter, stirring until smooth (batter will be thin).
  5. Bake: Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool: Allow cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
  7. Frost and Decorate: Once cooled, frost the top of one cake layer, place the second layer on top, and frost the entire cake. Decorate with fresh berries, sprinkles, or additional frosting as desired.

Serving Size: Makes one 2-layer cake, serving about 12 slices.

Enjoy your Chocolate Cake!
